Chapter 1: Reunions and New Beginnings

In Chapter 16, the college friends have graduated and moved on to various paths in life. However, they make a concerted effort to reunite for a special occasion, perhaps a class reunion or a friend's wedding. The chapter explores the joy of reuniting after some time apart, the updates they share about their lives, and the nostalgia for their college days. It also touches on the excitement of new beginnings and the realization that their friendships remain a constant source of support as they navigate the challenges of adulthood.
