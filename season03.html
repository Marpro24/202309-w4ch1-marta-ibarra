Ch.1 The Summer Break

In Episode 7, summer break arrives, and the college students disperse to different corners of the world. Some embark on internships, while others travel or return home. They stay connected through video calls, sharing their diverse experiences and adventures. This episode explores how the bonds they've formed continue to grow even when they're physically apart, as they support each other's endeavors and look forward to reuniting for the next semester.

Ch.2: New Horizons

In Episode 8, the students return to campus for the next academic year, bringing with them the wisdom and experiences gained over the summer. With a deeper understanding of each other and their shared goals, they set out to conquer new challenges. Some take on leadership roles in student organizations, while others dive into advanced coursework. This episode delves into their personal growth and the evolving dynamics within the group.

Ch.3: Facing Adversity

Episode 9 takes a more serious turn as the students encounter unexpected challenges. They support each other through difficult times, whether it's academic setbacks, personal crises, or global events that impact their lives. This episode showcases the strength of their friendships and the importance of having a support system during tough moments. It also emphasizes the resilience and determination of the group as they navigate these trials together.
