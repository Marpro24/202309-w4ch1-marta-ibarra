Ch.1: The Mid-College Crisis

In Chapter 10, the students find themselves at the midpoint of their college journey, facing the so-called "mid-college crisis." They reflect on their academic majors and career aspirations, questioning if they're on the right path. Some consider changing majors, while others seek internships and guidance from professors. This chapter explores the challenges of finding one's true calling and the support they offer each other during this period of self-discovery.

Chapter 11: A Semester Abroad

In Chapter 11, a few adventurous students decide to study abroad for a semester. They scatter across the globe, immersing themselves in new cultures and academic experiences. Through emails, letters, and social media, they share their international adventures with their friends back home. This chapter highlights the transformative power of travel and the excitement of reuniting with a fresh perspective when they return to campus.
